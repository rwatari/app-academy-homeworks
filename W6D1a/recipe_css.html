Ingredients

  • 5 cups vegetable or chicken stock
  • 2 Italian sausages, skin removed
  • 1/2 finely chopped onion
  • 1 clove minced garlic
  • 1 cup arborio rice (any starchy short grain rice will do)
  • 1/2 cup dry white wine or water
  • 2 tablespoons julienned sun dried tomatoes
  • grated parmesan cheese, salt, pepper to taste
  • chopped parsley for garnish

Simple sausage risotto recipe

Description

Risottos are simple to cook and very flexible. You can customize the dish however you want and it's great for using up leftovers. This recipe makes enough for 2 servings.

Directions

  1. Start heating your stock in a pot over low heat. Keeping the stock hot will speed up the cooking process later on.
  2. Set a non-stick skillet over medium-high heat and cook the sausage meat until browned, making sure to break up large clumps. Once the meat is cooked through, set it aside, keeping some of the rendered oil in the pan.
  3. Lower the heat to medium and add the olive oil. Sauté the minced onions and garlic for a few minutes until they start looking translucent. Add the rice and saute for a minute, until the rice starts releasing a nutty aroma.
  4. Add the wine or water and cook until it mostly evaporates, just under the level of the rice. Don't reduce too much or you risk burning the rice.
  5. Lower the heat to medium-low. In stages, add 1/2 cup of stock and cook until the liquid level is just above the level of the rice. No need to continuously stir. Swirling the pan around every time you add stock is enough to develop the starchy sauce.
  6. About 15 minutes in, just before the rice is done, add the sun dried tomatoes and sausage meat to heat up.
  7. You'll know the rice is done when it's translucent and there is just a bit of a bite in the center of the grain. When you run a spatula through the rice, it should take a second before the liquid and rice come to meet again. Turn off the heat and add the cheese, salt, and pepper to taste. Cover the pan and serve with parsley garnish after resting for 3 minutes.
